Ceremony

Saturday, September 1st, 2018
6:00 - 6:45 PM

The Harbor Room, Upstairs
8 Norwood Court
Gloucester, MA 01930

....................

The ceremony will consist of readings and music by family and friends. All guests are welcome to attend. Should you choose to skip the poetry and go straight to the party, no offense will be taken.

Free parking is available on site. When you arrive, one of the attendants will direct you to the next available space. Please bring your invitation (printed or digital) to show the attendant you are a guest.

Reception

Saturday, September 1st, 2018
7:00 - 10:30 PM

The Harbor Room, On The Dock
8 Norwood Court
Gloucester, MA 01930

....................

No need to follow a dress code. Heels and Havaianas are equally welcome! Depending on the weather, you may wish to bring a sweater or light coat for later in the evening.

A variety of Brazilian dishes will be served buffet-style. There will be options for veggie-lovers and meat-lovers alike.

The Schooner Festival fireworks begin at 9:15pm and should be visible from the venue.
